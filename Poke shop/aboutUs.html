So You Want To Know About Poke

The first thing to know is that poke is, and has always been, a restaurant catering to anyone that wants healthy food fast done their way.

The three shadowy figures who created poke wanted to make sure everyone had the freedom to good food that was still healthy. A place for families to bring their children. Poke has always understood that maintaining a healthy diet is difficult. What we know far more is that it shouldn't be!

The Right Tool For The Job

Using only locally sourced and fresh ingredients, poke allows you tasty and freeing experience with health eating. Beginning as a small chain and eventually to the renowned name that you know so well, Poke has always kept to its original promises

Our restaurants allow any customer to choose what food is right for them. Our environment designed to be calm yet fun, a place to relax and unwind with a nice snack or a buffet you never have to feel guilty about.
